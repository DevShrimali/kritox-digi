General Info

What is your typical project timeline?

Our timeline varies depending on the scope of the project. A typical corporate website takes 4-6 weeks, while complex web applications can take anywhere from 3 to 6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, absolutely. We believe that launching a project is just the beginning. We offer comprehensive AMC (Annual Maintenance Contracts) and retainer models to ensure your digital products remain secure, up-to-date, and optimized.

What tech stack do you specialize in?

We specialize in modern, scalable technologies. For the frontend, we use React, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS. For the backend, we rely on Node.js, Python, and robust cloud infrastructure like AWS and Google Cloud.

Can you integrate with our existing CRM/ERP?

Yes. Our engineering team is highly experienced in building custom API integrations for platforms like Salesforce, HubSpot, SAP, and various other enterprise systems.

Web Development

Are your websites SEO optimized?

Yes, all our websites are built with best practices for technical SEO, ensuring fast load times, proper semantic structure, and indexability across all search engines.

Do you use templates or custom designs?

We specialize in custom-designed solutions tailored to your brand's unique needs. We avoid pre-built templates to ensure your website is scalable, secure, and performs optimally.

Mobile Apps

Do you build for iOS or Android?

We build for both. We offer native development as well as cross-platform development using React Native and Flutter, ensuring you reach users on all devices efficiently.

Can you help publish the app to the App Store?

Yes! We handle the complete deployment process, from setting up developer accounts to managing the submission guidelines for both Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

UI/UX Design

What design tools do you use?

Our design team primarily uses Figma for creating high-fidelity wireframes, interactive prototypes, and design systems.

Do you offer UX auditing for existing websites?

Yes. We provide comprehensive UX audits where we analyze user behavior, pinpoint usability issues, and offer actionable recommendations to improve conversion rates and engagement.
