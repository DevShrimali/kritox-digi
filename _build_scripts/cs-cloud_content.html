Case Study

Cloud Migration & DevOps

Zero-downtime migration of massive on-premise infrastructure to the cloud.

The Challenge

A healthcare provider was operating on costly, aging on-premise servers that were becoming increasingly difficult to maintain and secure.

They needed to migrate their entire infrastructure to the cloud to reduce hardware costs and improve scalability, but strict healthcare compliance meant they couldn't afford a single minute of downtime or data loss during the transition.

"The migration was so flawless our internal staff didn't even notice when the switch to the cloud occurred."

The Solution

We designed a phased, zero-downtime migration strategy utilizing advanced containerization and CI/CD pipelines.

By containerizing their applications with Docker and orchestrating them via Kubernetes on Google Cloud, we created a redundant environment. Traffic was slowly routed to the new cloud infrastructure ensuring zero interruption to patient services.
