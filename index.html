Our Processes

We deliver a structured process for seamless project execution. From understanding your requirements to delivering the final solution, we ensure proactive technology services based on your business needs.

Discovery Meeting

The process begins with a discovery meeting discussing the client's goals and expectations in detail.

1

Requirement Gathering

Detailed requirements are systematically collected to ensure that the project's scope aligns precisely with the client's project needs.

2

Technical Consultation

Our subject matter experts (SMEs) provide an overview of the technical roadmap, outlining most suitable technologies to achieve the desired outcome.

3

Scope Document

A detailed scope specification document is created based on project requirements, detailing key deliverables, methodologies, along with detailed mind maps, activity diagrams, user role definitions, and a breakdown of functional and technical requirements.

4

Iterative Scope Refinement

The scope document undergoes iterative refinements based on clients feedback to ensure complete alignment with the client's expectations and vision.

5

Project Proposal

We present a final formal project proposal outlining the specifications, costs, timelines, key milestones and other deliverables of the project.

6

Agreement & Contract Signing

Once the proposal is approved, the terms are finalized and the contract is executed to formalize the engagement.

7

Project Onboarding

The project gets initiated with a clearly defined roadmap and a technical project manager who oversees the execution process.

8

Requirement Analysis

We analyze your specific resource needs, required skill sets, experience levels, and project duration.

1

Candidate Shortlisting

Our team handpicks the best-fit candidates from our pool of pre-vetted, top-tier developers.

2

Client Interviews

You interview the shortlisted candidates directly to ensure they align with your technical requirements and company culture.

3

Developer Onboarding

Once selected, the developer is seamlessly integrated into your team, tools, and communication channels.

4

Task Assignment & Workflow Setup

The developer begins working directly under your management, adapting to your specific agile workflows and daily tasks.

5

Regular Reporting & Monitoring

We ensure productivity through regular check-ins, performance monitoring, and transparent reporting.

6

Scaling & Support

You can easily scale your dedicated team up or down based on your evolving project requirements.

7

Sprint Planning

We collaboratively define the sprint goals and select user stories from the product backlog to be completed in the upcoming sprint.

1

Daily Stand-ups

Brief daily meetings are held to synchronize the team's activities, discuss progress, and identify any blockers.

2

Iterative Development

The team works cross-functionally to design, develop, and test the selected features within the time-boxed sprint.

3

Sprint Review

At the end of the sprint, we demonstrate the working software increment to stakeholders and gather valuable feedback.

4

Sprint Retrospective

The team reflects on the past sprint to identify continuous improvement opportunities for their workflow and collaboration.

5

Continuous Integration & Delivery

Code is continuously integrated and tested, ensuring that every iteration results in a potentially shippable product.
