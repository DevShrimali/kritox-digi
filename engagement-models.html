Dedicated Development Team

A collaborative approach where a client hires a group of skilled developers from a software development company. This team works exclusively on the client's project, offering flexibility, scalability, and an extended arm to the client's existing workforce. It's an ideal model for projects that demand a high level of engagement, long-term commitment, and frequent changes in requirements. This model allows clients to manage the development process directly, ensuring transparency, control, and the ability to align resources according to the project's evolving needs. Kritox Digital’s flexible dedicated model lets you hire pre-vetted, experienced developers of your choice.

  • Tailor the team’s size and skill sets as per project needs.
  • Directly manage and oversee the project’s progress.
  • Easily scale up or down the team based on evolving requirements.
  • Pay for the actual resources utilized, optimizing budget allocation.
  • Ideal for ongoing or complex projects with changing needs.
  • Seamless integration with in-house teams fosters synergy.
  • Exclusive focus on your project ensures quicker delivery.
  • Access to a specialized team with diverse skill sets.
  • Clear communication and regular updates on project status.
  • Ensure confidentiality and retention of Intellectual Property Rights.

Time and Material (T&M)

The Time and Material (T&M) model is a dynamic engagement strategy that offers flexibility and adaptability for projects with frequently changing requirements. In this model, clients pay for the actual time and resources invested in the project. It suits endeavors where the scope, specifications, or deadlines are prone to alterations during development. This model grants the freedom to modify project objectives, implement new features, and adjust priorities as the project progresses. Engaging with Kritox Digital gives you the freedom to pay only for what goes into your development process.

  • Adaptable to changing project requirements, allowing adjustments as the project progresses.
  • Provides clarity in cost breakdowns based on actual time and resources utilized.
  • Ideal for projects with evolving scopes or undefined requirements.
  • Clients have greater control over the project to prioritize and modify tasks.
  • Monitor project progress closely and make course corrections accordingly.
  • Enables efficient resource management according to the project’s immediate needs.
  • Eliminates the need for a fully defined scope initially, enabling the project to start promptly.
  • Detailed records of work hours and incurred costs offer comprehensive insights.

Fixed Cost Model

The Fixed Cost Model in software development is a structured approach where the project scope, deliverables, and costs are predetermined and agreed upon before the project commences. This model is suitable for projects with well-defined requirements and limited potential for changes in the scope. Under this model, the client and the service provider establish a fixed price for the entire project, providing financial predictability. It offers clarity in terms of project timelines, costs, and deliverables, allowing clients to plan budgets more accurately.

  • Clear understanding and agreement on the project cost before commencement.
  • Clearly outlined project scope and deliverables limit potential changes and scope creep.
  • Allows for better budget planning and management with fixed costs.
  • Clearly defined project timelines and deadlines.
  • Minimizes risks for clients, as the service provider bears the risk of any budget overruns.
  • Provides clarity on project milestones, requirements, and deliverables.
  • Efficient planning for resources, schedules, and project management due to a fixed scope and cost.
