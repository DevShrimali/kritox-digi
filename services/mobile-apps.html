1. What digital services do you provide?
We provide full-scale digital services including brand strategy, UI/UX design, custom web and mobile app development, e-commerce integration, cloud solutions, and digital marketing.
2. How do you ensure high-quality delivery?
We follow strict Agile methodologies, conduct automated and manual QA testing, perform comprehensive peer code reviews, and maintain direct, transparent communication channels throughout every sprint.
3. Do you support flexible hiring models?
Yes, we offer fixed-price projects, time and material setups, and dedicated team extensions so you can easily scale up or down based on your business requirements.
4. How is intellectual property (IP) handled?
Your project and codebase are 100% yours. We sign comprehensive NDAs before starting and transfer all intellectual property and source code ownership upon project completion.
5. What post-launch support do you offer?
We provide 30 to 90 days of free support and maintenance following any major release to resolve any issues and ensure smooth operation of your software across all platforms.
